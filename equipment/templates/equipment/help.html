Frequently Asked Questions

For Renters
How does the rental process work?

Our rental process is simple: browse our available equipment online, select your desired items and dates, reserve them through your account, and pick them up at our location. After your activity, return the equipment to our shop by the end of the rental period.

What if I damage the equipment?

We understand that accidents happen. Minor wear and tear is expected and covered in your rental fee. For significant damage, we assess it when you return the equipment. We recommend adding rental insurance at checkout which covers most damage for a small additional fee.

How do I know what size equipment I need?

For most equipment, we provide sizing guides on each product page. Generally, ski length depends on your height and skill level, while boots are based on your shoe size. When you pick up your equipment, our staff will ensure everything fits properly before you leave.

Can I extend my rental period?

Yes, rental extensions are possible subject to equipment availability. Contact us before your rental period ends to request an extension. Additional days will be charged at the standard daily rate.

Do you offer seasonal rentals?

Yes, we offer seasonal rentals for most equipment types. Seasonal rentals typically run from November through April and provide significant savings compared to multiple short-term rentals. Contact us directly or check our seasonal rental page for more information.

For Equipment Managers
How do I list my equipment for rental?

To become an equipment manager on our platform, you'll need to register for a manager account and go through our verification process. Once approved, you can add your inventory through your dashboard. You'll need to provide details like equipment type, brand, model, condition, size options, and high-quality photos.

How are rental fees determined and paid?

As an equipment manager, you set your own daily, weekly, and seasonal rental rates. Our platform charges a small commission on each completed rental. Payments are processed securely through our system, and funds are transferred to your account automatically after the rental period concludes and the equipment is confirmed returned.

What happens if my equipment is damaged?

Our platform includes a damage protection policy. Minor wear and tear is considered normal, but for significant damage, you can document the issue when equipment is returned. If the renter has insurance, the repair or replacement costs are covered. Otherwise, you can file a claim through our system, and we'll help facilitate the resolution between you and the renter.

How do I manage multiple rental requests?

Your manager dashboard provides a comprehensive system to track all incoming rental requests, active rentals, and equipment availability. You can review, approve, or decline requests from this dashboard, and our system automatically prevents double-bookings for the same equipment. You'll receive notifications for new requests, upcoming returns, and any issues that need your attention.

Can I offer both pickup and delivery options?

Yes, you can customize your service options. You can offer in-store pickup and return, delivery to specific locations (like nearby hotels or resorts), or even home delivery within a service area you define. Each option can have different fees associated with it, and you can adjust your availability calendar to manage your schedule effectively.
